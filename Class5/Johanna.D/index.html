Statistics On Mental Health

A lot of people have poor mental health. Here are the statistics on mental health.

  • About 42.5 million Americans claim to suffer from Anxiety disorders making it the highest reported mental health issue in the US.

  • Symptoms of mental illnesses start showing up around the age of 14.

  • About 1 in 4 American adults suffers from a diagnosable mental disorder in a given year, and one in 10 will suffer from a depressive illness, such as major depression or bipolar disorder

  • Mental health crises account for 60 million visits to primary care and 6 million ER visits annually (Centers for Diasease Control and Prevention)
