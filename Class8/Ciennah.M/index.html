Ever wanted to know completely useless and or fake minecraft facts? This part is for you.

    Fake Facts

  • Acacia wood makes pumpkins grow faster.
  • You can make a portal with glowstone and water.
  • Eating a uncooked porkchop and then drinking potion of harming will make your health bar turn green.
  • You can get curse of binding armor off if you use a spash potion of invisibility.
  • If you give an iron golem a rose it won't attack you if you hit it.
  • You can tame a dog with steak.

    Useless Facts

  • Cats don't take fall damage
  • Creepers are based on a failed model of a pig
  • Both types of spiders can't climb up ice
  • A splash potion of harming will heal a skeleton and a splash potion of healing will harm a skeleton
  • You can't see a nether portal behind water or ice
  • Using a tool for a different use than it is intended to will decrease it's durability by 2 points instead of 1


Knowing some Minecraft facts is helpful in some cases. For example, did you know that on Bedrock edition, you can make an infinite potion. To do this, put the potion you want in a cauldron, then you put water above the place the dripstone is gonna be, last thing you do is place the blocks that block the water off and place the dripstone above the cauldron.
