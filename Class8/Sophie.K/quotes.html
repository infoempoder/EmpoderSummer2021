Here are the 8 Quotes

Remember That Quotes Can Be Interpreted However You See Them!

Home Quotes About Resources


  1. "It's not the load that breaks you down. It's the way you carry it."
    -C.S. Lewis
    This quote, by British Writer C.S. Lewis might motivate you to try and do something differently that might be hurting you.




  2. "Ride on! Rough-shod if need be, smooth-shod if that will do, but ride on! Ride on over all the obstacles, and win the race!"
    -Charles Dickens
    These words by Writer Charles Dickens can be the inspiration to continue pushing yourself to a goal whether it is hard or easy.


  3. "Winners never quit and quitters never win"
    -Vince Lombardi
    You can only win and reach a goal if you try, is what this precept by Football Coach Vince Lombardi depicts.

  4. "Use what talent you possess: the woods would be very silent if no birds sang except those that sang best."
    -Henry van Dyke
    This quote by American Author Henry van Dyke may influence you to persue or try out a talent you have.

  5. "Whether you believe you can or believe you can't, you are absolutely right."
    -Henry Ford
    These words spoken by American Industrialist Henry Ford show the importance of believing in yourself.

  6. "When you come to the end of your rope, tie a knot in it and hang on."
    -Thomas Jefferson
    This precept by the 3rd U.S. President Thomas Jefferson is a push to not give up when it feels like the end of something.

  7. "If your ship doesn't come in, swim out to it."
    -Jonathan Winters
    You have to work toward something when it doesn't come right to you, is what this quote by American Comedian Jonathon Winters portrays.

  8. "Sometimes rejection in life is really redirection."
    -Tavis Smiley
    This last quote by Talk Show Host Tavis Smiley explains that sometimes rejection is best.
