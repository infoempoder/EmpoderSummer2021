Just a disclaimer, you DON'T need supplements to reach your goals, they are to supplement your workout regiment - help to reach goals faster.

Protein

  1. recommended 15%-30% of your calories come from protein
  2. Helps curb your appetite by reducing levels of the hunger hormone gherkin, all while increasing levels of appetite-reducing hormones like glucagon-like peptide-I (GLP-I), peptide YY (PYY) and cholecystokinin (CCK)
  3. If your in a caloric surplus take it in addition with a meal, for caloric deficit, take it in replacement of a snack
  4. Benefits:
    • Convenient and yummy source of protein
    • essential for muscle growth
    • helps muscles recover faster
    • can be used as a snack
    • Easy to make!
