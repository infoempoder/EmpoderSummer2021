Ways to stop animal abuse

Ways to stop animal abuse is if u suspect or observe that a dog is being abused then contact local animal welfare agency or report it to your local animal control, law enforcement or human organization. You can also support the SPCA (Society for the prevention of cruelty to animals) or a dog shelter. Save abused animals

  • 2nd Dogs may have food aggression toward people or animals, seperation anxiety, and fear responses.
