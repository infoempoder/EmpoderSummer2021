The Many Genders

Most people believe that there are only two genders, male and female,and no inbetween. But gender is a spectrum and there are actually over 70 genders!

    • Male/Female
      Someone who identifies as one of the two genders in the gender binary
    • Non-binary
      Someone who identifies outside of the gender binary
    • Agender
      Someone who identifies as not having a specific gender
    • Genderfluid
      Someone whose gender changes over time
    • Omnigender
      Someone who identifies as most, but not all, genders
    • Pangender
      Someone who identifies as all genders
    • Demi-Gender/Boy/Girl
      Someone who partially identifes as two or more genders
    • Bigender
      Someone who has two distinct gender identities
    • Xenogender
      Someone whose gender can only be fully described by how they relate to different things that usually dont have anything to do with gender.
    • Genderflux
      Someone whose gender fluctuates in intensity.
