Dolores Huerta was born April 10 1930 in New Mexico. At an early age she saw her dads will to be someone in life, that she went to Stockton College to continue her education. She saw the struggles farm workers families that she decided to help them. And that was the beggining of her carrer. She meet Cesar Chavez and both created United Farm Workers Union and both continued their work to fight the unjustice the farm workers were facing.

Sonia Sotomayor - She was born June 25th 1954 in New York. She came from a modest family but saw injustice in a tv show, in where she was inspired to become a judge. She struggled to achieve good grades,she pushed herself to succeed. After many struggles on August 2009 she made history by becoming the first Latina Supreme Court Justice in US History. She is known for protecting minoritites, womens health and crimminal justice.
