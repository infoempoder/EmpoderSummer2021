The Equal Pay Act of 1963 - When world war two began, men enlisted in the military caused a shortage of workers, women took over the job of the men with less pay for the same job . In 1945 Congress introduced the Womens Equal Pay Act, that allowed equal pay between men and women however it was never approved. President John F Kennedy signed the Equal Pay act in 1963.
