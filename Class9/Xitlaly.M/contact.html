Depression


Depression is another common and serious mental illness that negativly affects how you feel,think,and act. Making you lose interest in things you once enjoyed and decreasing your ability to function at work and work.It can cause serious consequences and requries long-term treatment.Most teens depression symptoms ease with treatment like medical and psychological counseling.


What causes Depression?

  • Issues that negitivly impact self esteem,like obesity,peer problems,long-term bullying, or acadimic problems.
  • Victim or witness of violence.
  • Being in the LGBTQ+ comunity in a unspupportive enviroment Low self esteem,being overly dependent,self critical or pessimistic
  • Blood related to someone who has depression or bipolar disorder.

Ways to Cope

  1. Reach out and stay connected.You may be tired and think it's a sign of weekness or you're being a burden. It's really not, there are people who really care about you and want to help. Talk to a close friend,realative or loved one.

  2. Do things you enjoy. Although you can't force yourself to have fun, you can push youself to do things. It feels pretty good once you do you'd be suprised.Maybe you can express youself through,art or music.

  3. Get up and Get Moving.Yes getting up from bed may be tiring also not only that but working out. Working out is important because it's a powerful depression fighter.It's recommended to do 30 minutes a day but you can always start off small, maybe for 10 or 15 minutes.

  4. Eat healty.It's good to prevent thing that have caffeine,alcohol,trans fats and foods with high levels of chemical preservatives or hormones, like certain meats.Don't skip meals it can make you feel irritable and tired.Minimize sugar and refined carbs.like confort foods they quickly lead in to a crash in mood.
