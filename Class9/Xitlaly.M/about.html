Anxiety


As you may know anxiety is the most common mental illness. Affecting 25% of all teens and 30% of all teen girls. Experts are seeing a rise in the level of anxiety, found both in adults and teens.It's your bodys natural response to stress.You may experince it when it's the first day of school,job interveiw,giving a speech,test,etc.An anxiety disorder at all time preventing you from doing things you enjoy.Maybe it prevent you from doing every day things like going outside,going into a elevator,or crossing a sidewalk.That's a extreme case,it can get worse if left untreated.


What Causes Anxiety?

  • Traumatic events
  • Stress due to an illness
  • Stress build up
  • Other mental health disorders
  • Blood related to someone who has anxiety

Ways to Cope

  1. Get some sleep.By not getting enough sleep can worsen you anxiety,by anxiety can cause sleeping problems.It's also recomended to go time bed at the same time each night.

  2. Exercise regularly.It let's out brain chemicals to reduce the body's reaction to pain and stress,also feeling of euphoria or happiness.Five minutes of aerobic exercise can start the anti-anxiety effects.

  3. Focus on something less Anxiety provoking.It's helpful to focus on something else other than your anxiety.Maybe do some house work,or activity you enjoy or hobby. Some ideas are: orginizing around the house,a creative activity like painting or drawing,liten to music,read a good book,or a funny movie.
