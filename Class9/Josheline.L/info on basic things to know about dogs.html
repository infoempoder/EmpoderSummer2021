Info you need to know about your dog these are basic things you need to know:

Normal temperture for Dogs:

Normal body temperature for dogs and cats is 101.0 to 102.5°F (38.3 to 39.2°C).But if your pet's temperature rises above 104°F (40.0°C) or falls below 99°F (37.2°C), take your pet to your veterinarian.

How can Dogs get sick:

"Dogs throw up for a variety of reasons. Dog vomiting is often brought on by a sudden change in diet, through scavenging, such as feeding off human leftovers, or from motion sickness or even eating too much or too quickly."These sorce is found in "Can Dogs Get the Flu? What Is Canine Flu? - Pet WebMD". Over all they can get sick by cold tempertaures and with another dog infection.

Routine for properly caring for dogs:

  • Exercise: In general a dog should geta 30 minutes and 2 hours of exercise every day. It just depends on how big it is.
  • Nutrition: "The six basic nutrients are water, proteins, fats, carbohydrates, minerals, and vitamins." These source can be found in "Nutrition - General Feeding Guidelines for Dogs | VCA Animal".
  • Living space:A dog needs their space to run around just if they can't go on walks because they don't have all their vaccines or just in general have their space.

Causes of Illness:

"Oral infections are actually the most common dog disease and tend to become an issue in dogs after the age of three. Tartar and gingivitis are among the most common oral issues while the more severe periodontal diseases, such as abscesses tend to occur more often in older dogs in the later stages of their life." These source can be found in "The Most Common Dog Illnesses: Symptoms and Treatment" website.
