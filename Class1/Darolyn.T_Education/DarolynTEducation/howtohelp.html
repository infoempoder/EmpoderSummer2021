How To Help



Right now the fight for education for children is still a ongoing battle that still has a long way to go. Some problems are not in our control but there are still alot of things we as people can do to help.

What has been done so far

Currently there are many many organizations set in place to help kids get to a better place in their lives by working directly with the countries. Aside from that many organizations have made it a goal to support these kids in not only financial aid but in other ways as well such as getting them in a safer enviorment.


What can you do to help

Here are a few ideas on what you can do to help

  1. Donate!: There are many great organizations that would put that money to changing the world. You can also donate things like books, notebooks, pens, penicls, and other utilities such as food or clean water. Look in the "Resource" tab to find websites you can donate to.


  2. Volunteer Programs: There are a few volunteer programs that are available wether you want to help from where you are or want to go and help people right from the source. A little bit a research on this could go a long way!


  3. Support: Sometimes the best you can do is to just support these programs and this cause. Giving your support can be just a meaningful as anything else and more.
