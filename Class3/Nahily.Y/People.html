Maya Angelou

Maya Angelou was the first female inaugural poet in U.S. presidential history.She also the first African American poet and first female poet to participate in a recitation for a U.S. president's inauguration.

Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela won the Nobel Prize for Peace in 1993 and he was also the first black president of South America and he fought for apartheid rights.
