Stress is when you are overwhelmed by something. For example you have multiple things on your mind you can't concentrate on one thing beause your thinking about another. You can also be stressed when your being pressured to do something. Being stressed is a way your body reacts when it's faced with a challenge or something demanding. You mainly get stressed when you feel angry, nervous or frustrated.
