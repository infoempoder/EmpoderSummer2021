Get to Recycling!


Some great ways to recycle are:

  • Putting an extra bin for glass and plastic materials, it is helpful to sort recyclable materials from trash because it helps reduce the amount of trash that needs processing.

  • You can also recycle by fixing things that dont work instead of replacing them.

  • Another way you can recycle is reusing grocery bags, class jars,plastic containers, and cardboard boxes. This helps by decreasing the amount of waste.
